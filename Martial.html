Jiu-jitsu

Jiu-jitsu is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on ground fighting and submission holds. It emphasizes technique and leverage to overcome larger opponents. It is commonly used in mixed martial arts (MMA) and self-defense scenarios.

Karate

Karate is a traditional martial art originating from Japan. It emphasizes striking techniques such as punches, kicks, and knee strikes. Karate practitioners also learn self-defense, forms (katas), and sparring techniques.

Judo

Judo is a martial art and Olympic sport that focuses on throws and grappling techniques. It emphasizes using an opponent's strength and momentum against them. Judo practitioners learn techniques for taking down opponents and immobilizing them on the ground.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, also known as "The Art of Eight Limbs," is a combat sport from Thailand. It utilizes strikes with fists, elbows, knees, and shins. Muay Thai training involves a combination of techniques, conditioning, and sparring to develop power, speed, and agility.
